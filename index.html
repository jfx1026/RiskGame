How to Play

Goal

Conquer all territories on the map to win.

Your Turn

  • Tap your territory to select it (shown with a black border)
  • Tap an enemy neighbor (pulsing white border) to attack
  • Attack as many times as you want, then tap End Turn

Combat

  • Both sides roll dice based on army count
  • Higher rolls win - attacker needs to beat defender
  • Loser loses one army per die comparison
  • Conquer a territory when its armies reach zero

Reinforcements

At the end of your turn, you receive new armies based on how many territories you control.

Tips

  • Attack with more armies for better odds
  • Don't spread your armies too thin
  • Use Fast Forward to speed up AI turns
